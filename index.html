Towers Of Hanoi


Number of Moves:

4
3
2
1

Game Instructions:
  • Only one stone may be moved at a time. Double click the row/stack to remove the last stone.
  • Place the stone on top of another stack or on an empty row. Do this by single clicking on the new stack.
  • No stone may be placed on top of a stone that is smaller than it.
  • In order to win the game you must place ALL 4 stones on either the red or yellow rows/stacks and in the same order from left to right (4,3,2,1).
